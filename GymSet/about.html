Our History

Founded in 2005, GymSet has been a pillar of the fitness community for over 15 years. It all began with a small fitness studio and a vision to empower individuals to transform their lives through fitness and wellness.

Over the years, GymSet has grown into a state-of-the-art fitness facility with a passionate team of trainers and a diverse range of fitness programs. We continue to evolve and adapt to meet the changing needs of our members, helping them achieve their fitness goals every step of the way.
